# 📖 Welcome to DreamBeesAI: Your Creative Journey

Hello! Welcome to the **DreamBeesAI** hive. This guide is designed to help you get started with your new AI creative companion, even if you're not a technical expert.

---

## 🎨 Phase 1: The "Dreaming" Loop

DreamBeesAI isn't just a chat app; it's a collaborative art studio. The core of the experience is **Dreaming**—generating images using the powerful Gemini 3.1 model.

### 1. Simple Messaging
Just type a description of what you want to see.
> *Example: "A futuristic city built inside a giant glass honey pot, neon lights, 4K."*

### 2. High-Quality 2x2 Grids
If you enable **Grid Mode**, DreamBeesAI will generate four variations of your prompt and present them in a beautiful 2x2 collage. This is perfect for exploring different artistic directions quickly!

### 3. Iterative Editing
You can follow up on a previous image.
> *Example: "Now make it night time and add some flying bees."*

---

## 🌐 Phase 2: Synced Reality (Discord & Telegram)

One of the coolest things about DreamBeesAI is that it lives where you do.

-   **Discord Studio**: When you message the bot in your designated channel, it creates a private "Art Studio" thread just for you. Every image you generate there is also saved to the Web interface!
-   **Telegram Resonance**: Chat with the bot on Telegram and watch your creative history sync instantly with your local dashboard.

---

## 🧠 Phase 3: The AI Memory (BroccoliDB)

DreamBeesAI has a "Long-Term Memory" called the **Cognitive Substrate**.

-   **Why it matters**: The bot remembers your recent artistic style and conversations. This creates a "Resonance" that makes the AI feel like it truly understands your creative vision over time.
-   **Privacy First**: All these memories are stored **locally** on your server. Your creative journey is yours alone.

---

## 📊 Phase 4: Monitoring the Hive

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see some "Hive Metrics":

-   **Stability**: If this is high (e.g., 0.99), the AI's memory is healthy and consistent.
-   **Entropy**: This represents the "randomness" or "creative chaos" in the system. A little entropy is good for creativity!
-   **Node Count**: This is simply how many "thoughts" or messages the AI is currently remembering.
