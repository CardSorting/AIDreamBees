# 🍪 DreamBeesAI Recipes: Quick Success Guide

Sometimes, you just want to see a masterpiece *now*. These recipes are designed to get you there in three easy steps.

---

## 🎨 Recipe 1: Your First Masterpiece

Perfect for when you're just starting and want to see what the AI can do!

1.  **Open the Web Dashboard**: Visit `http://localhost:5173`.
2.  **Toggle "Grid Mode"**: Make sure the colorful grid icon is active at the top.
3.  **The Prompt**: Type "A majestic honey bee made of liquid gold flying through a cosmic nebula, 8K resolution, cinematic lighting."

> [!TIP]
> Use the "Grid Mode" to see four different angles at once. It's the fastest way to find a style you love!

---

## ⚡ Recipe 2: Resonance-Sync Your Discord

Ever wanted to generate art on the go and see it on your big screen later?

1.  **Set Up the Discord Bot**: Follow the [Configuration Guide](CONFIGURATION.md) to get your `DISCORD_TOKEN`.
2.  **Start the Backend**: Run `npm start` in the `backend/` folder.
3.  **Chat with the Bot**: Message your bot on Discord. It will create a "Studio" thread for you. Tell it, "Dream: A cyberpunk beehive."

> [!IMPORTANT]
> When you get back to your computer and open the Web UI, your Discord dream will be right there waiting for you!

---

## 🧪 Recipe 3: Fine-Tuning the Entropy

If you're feeling adventurous and want the AI to get "wilder" with its ideas!

1.  **Observe the Hive Status**: Look at the "Stability" and "Entropy" meters at the top of the interface.
2.  **Start a Technical Conversation**: Ask the AI, "How is the cognitive substrate today?"
3.  **Push the Limits**: Tell the AI to "Increase the entropy of the next generation," and then provide a creative prompt.

> [!NOTE]
> High entropy can lead to abstract, unpredictable, and often stunning results. It's the "Creative Chaos" of the hive!
