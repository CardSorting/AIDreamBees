# 📖 DreamBeesAI Glossary: Translating the Technical

Sometimes we use some big words to describe how DreamBeesAI works. This guide is here to help you understand what they mean in plain English!

---

## 🧠 The "Digital Brain" (Substrate)

-   **Cognitive Substrate**: Think of this as the AI's **Digital Memory**. It's where the AI stores everything it's learned about your current project and conversation.
-   **BroccoliDB**: This is the specific name of the technology that powers the AI's memory. It's fast, local, and private.
-   **Merkle-Reasoning DAG**: A fancy way of saying a **"Tree of Thoughts."** Instead of a simple list, the AI organizes its memories like branches on a tree, helping it stay consistent.

---

## ⚡ Real-Time Effects (Resonance)

-   **Resonance**: When the AI's memory and your local chat are in perfect sync. It means the AI "feels" your presence across the web, Discord, and Telegram simultaneously.
-   **Entropy**: This is a measure of **"Creative Chaos."** 
    -   *Low Entropy*: The AI stays very safe and predictable.
    -   *High Entropy*: The AI gets more creative, random, and experimental.
-   **Thinking State**: When you see "Thinking" in the app, it means the AI is currently "visualizing" its response before it actually sends it to you.

---

## 🖼️ Image Generation (The Dreaming Loop)

-   **Multimodal**: "Multimodal" simply means the AI can understand and create multiple types of things—specifically **text and images**.
-   **Z-Image-Turbo (ZIT)**: A special high-speed mode for generating images quickly.
-   **Grid Mode**: A feature that asks the AI to generate four different variations of your idea and combine them into one 2x2 picture.
-   **SynthID**: An invisible "digital signature" that proves the image was created by an AI, ensuring responsible use.

---

## 🌐 Connectivity (The Hive)

-   **Soketi**: The engine that powers the real-time messages. It's like the "Wi-Fi" specifically for your AI's thoughts.
-   **Orchestrator**: A specialized "translator" that takes messages from Discord or Telegram and makes sure they fit into the AI's memory correctly.
